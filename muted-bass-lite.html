Muted Bass Lite

Muted Bass Lite is the free version of Muted Bass, a sampled Sterling Ray 35 played palm muted and DI, ready to be plugged into your favourite signal chain. Perfect for jazz, funk, edm and pop, the round and rich tone of this instrument will be a nice touch in many occasions.

Download Muted Bass Lite (Free)

Features:

  • 246 high-quality samples (instead of 492)
  • 1 velocity layer (instead of 2)
  • 2 round robins (instead of 4)
  • Dead notes and slap noises
  • Adjustable Release Triggers
  • No intelligent Release Triggers
  • No scripted legato
