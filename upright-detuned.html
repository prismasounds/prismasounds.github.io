Upright Detuned

Upright Detuned was born by sampling an old detuned piano sitting for years in Leo Val's film school in Finland. The piano's sound was unstable, the sustain very short and the lid made all sorts of noises, which gave it a peculiar eerie quality.

The result is a wispy, lively, whimsical instrument perfect for any haunting situation. Whether you're scoring a horror film, creating atmospheric ambient music, or adding character to your compositions, Upright Detuned delivers that perfect touch of the supernatural.

Loot Audio $19 KVR Audio $19 Gumroad $19

Features:

  • 5 round robins;
  • A fully controllable ghostly drone layer;
  • Release triggers;
  • Adjustable ghost noises;
  • Built-in reverb with 3 custom IRs;
  • Built-in delay, saturation and rotator effects;
  • UI element design by Leo Val.
